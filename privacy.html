Privacy Policy — Buildminm

Last updated: October 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Buildminm (“we”, “our”, “Buildminm”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the BUILD website and services.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide services and process orders. This may include:

  • Contact information (name, email) provided voluntarily by you when ordering.
  • Payment confirmations and transaction IDs (for manual verification).
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type for security and analytics.

2. How we use your information

We use information to: process purchases, deliver digital content, verify payments, prevent fraud, and improve our services. We do not sell personal data.

3. KYC and verification

For large donations or withdrawals, we may request identity documents to comply with anti-fraud and regulatory requirements. These documents are stored securely and accessed only by authorized BUILD personnel.

4. Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand site usage and improve the user experience. Cookies can be disabled in the browser settings but certain features may not work correctly.

5. How we protect your data

We take reasonable measures to secure stored data (encrypted storage, restricted access). For digital downloads we use time-limited links to prevent unauthorized sharing.

6. Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with payment providers or legal authorities if required by law.

7. Retention

We retain transaction and KYC records for the period required by applicable laws and for operational needs.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact Buildminm Support at support@buildminm.example. Replace this address with your preferred contact email.

By using Buildminm services you acknowledge this policy. We may update this policy and will date it accordingly.
